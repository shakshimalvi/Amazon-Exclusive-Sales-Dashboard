# Amazon Exclusive Sales Dashboard

A professional **Power BI** interactive dashboard analyzing e-commerce sales performance for **Amazon Exclusive** (fictional dataset). This dashboard provides clear insights into revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and product performance across regions and categories.

### 📊 Key Features & Insights

- **Executive KPIs**: 
  - Total Revenue: **₹32.87M**
  - Total Profit: **₹1.64M**
  - Total Orders: **50,000**
  - Quantity Sold: **150,000**
  - Average Rating: **3.00**

- **Category Performance**: Beauty leads in profit, followed by Books, Fashion, Electronics & Home & Kitchen
- **Trend Analysis**: Revenue and Profit growth trends from 2022 to 2023
- **Regional Analysis**: Revenue and profit distribution across North America, Europe, Asia, Middle East, etc.
- **Payment Insights**: Breakdown by Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, Wallet, Cash on Delivery
- **Customer Behavior**: Review count vs Revenue, Discount % impact on Revenue & Profit
- **Product & Rating Analysis**: Relationship between ratings and revenue

### 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **Power BI Desktop**
- DAX Measures & Calculations
- Advanced Visualizations (Bar, Line, Pie, Matrix, Scatter)
- Interactive Slicers (Region, Category, Year)

### 🎯 Purpose

This project showcases strong **Business Intelligence & Data Visualization** skills, ideal for e-commerce analytics, sales reporting, and data-driven decision making.
